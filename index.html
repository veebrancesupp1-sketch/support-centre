FAQ

Common questions

MetaMask wallet access is tied entirely to your Secret Recovery Phrase (seed phrase). If you have your 12-word phrase, you can restore your wallet on any device using "Import wallet." If you've lost it, contact our support team — we'll walk you through every available recovery option and help you understand what data might still be accessible.

Stuck withdrawals on Binance are usually caused by network congestion, security holds, or pending KYC verification. First, check the withdrawal status in your Transaction History tab. If it shows "Processing" for more than 24 hours, or if it's marked "Failed" but funds haven't returned, reach out to us with your TXID and we'll investigate directly.

Coinbase may restrict accounts for incomplete identity verification, unusual login activity, compliance reviews, or suspected policy violations. Check your registered email for any notices from Coinbase. Our team can help you understand the reason, prepare appeal documentation, and communicate effectively with Coinbase's support team on your behalf.

Our typical first-response time is under 2 hours during business hours, and under 6 hours at any other time. For critical issues like suspected account compromise or large missing funds, we prioritize immediately. Fill out the contact form below and include as much detail as possible — this significantly speeds up resolution.

Never share your seed phrase with anyone, including us. Legitimate support teams will never ask for your secret recovery phrase or private keys. Our support process involves guiding you through steps you perform yourself, on your own devices. If anyone claiming to be support is requesting your seed phrase, that is a scam — report it immediately.
